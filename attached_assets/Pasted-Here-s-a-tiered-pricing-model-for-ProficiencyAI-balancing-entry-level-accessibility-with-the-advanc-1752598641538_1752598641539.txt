Here’s a tiered pricing model for ProficiencyAI, balancing entry-level accessibility with the advanced capabilities institutions need. All prices are in USD and billed monthly, with a 15% discount on annual pre-payment.

Tier	Price Per User / Month	Key Features	Limits & Add-Ons
Starter	Free	• Manual quiz & testbank creation
• Up to 5 quizzes
• Basic reporting	• Testbank max 100 questions
• AI-generate up to 10 questions/month
• No CAT or proctoring
Basic	$20 (or $204/yr)	• Unlimited manual quizzes & testbanks
• AI-generate up to 200 questions/month
• CSV export	• AI-validation up to 50 questions/month
• No adaptive testing, no proctoring, no LTI
Professional	$50 (or $510/yr)	All Basic, plus:
• Computer-Adaptive Testing
• LTI integration (Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle)
• Unlimited AI validation

• Extra sessions $5/session
Institutional	$1,200 flat / month	All Professional, plus:
• Seat-based pricing for up to 100 users
• Cohort & group management
• Instructor & admin roles	• Additional seats: $10/user/month

Enterprise	Custom	All Institutional, plus:
• SSO & SCIM user-provisioning
• Dedicated account manager & SLA
• White-labeling
• Custom integrations & on-prem options	• Volume discounts

• Premium support & training

Feature Breakdown by Tier
Manual Quiz & Testbank Management
• Create, tag, and organize testbanks with Bloom’s taxonomy, learning objectives & custom categories.
• Import/export CSV/Excel.

AI-Powered Question Generation & Validation
• Generate brand-new items from your references or open web.
• Validation agent to flag ambiguous or poorly performing questions.

Computer-Adaptive Testing (CAT)
• Real-time item selection based on learner ability estimate (Professional+).
• Customizable stopping rules and score reporting.

Proctoring
• Secure browser lockdown and identity verification.
• Session recordings and audit logs.

LTI Integration
• Seamless embedding & grade passback with Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, D2L, etc.

Analytics & Reporting
• Item-analysis (difficulty, discrimination), learner dashboards, cohort comparisons.

Enterprise-Grade Services
• SSO/SCIM, data-residency options, custom workflows, priority support.

Optional Add-Ons (All Plans)
Extra AI-Generation Credits: $10 per 100-question block

Additional Proctoring Sessions: as noted per tier

Custom API Access & Webhooks: $200/month

Notes on Implementation:

Annual Billing: 15% discount automatically applied.

Seat Bundles: Institutional & Enterprise tiers support bulk seat purchases.

Onboarding & Training: One-time setup fee available for Professional+ plans.

This model lets solo educators and small teams start free, scales to power large programs, and provides a clear upgrade path as your needs (and user count) grow.
