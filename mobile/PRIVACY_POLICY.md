## Introduction

Welcome to ProficiencyAI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using ProficiencyAI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using the App:

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, password, role (teacher/student), institutional affiliation
- **Profile Information:** Profile photo, bio, educational background, teaching subjects
- **Contact Information:** Phone number (optional), alternative email addresses
- **Payment Information:** Billing address, payment method details (processed securely by Stripe; we do not store credit card numbers)

### 1.2 Content You Create

- **Exam Content:** Questions, answers, explanations, images, and other materials you create
- **Test Banks:** Collections of questions and assessments
- **Student Responses:** Answers submitted during exams
- **Feedback and Comments:** Reviews, ratings, and comments on questions or exams

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
- **Usage Data:** Features accessed, time spent in app, navigation paths, button clicks
- **Log Data:** IP address, app version, timestamps, error logs, crash reports
- **Performance Data:** App loading times, feature performance metrics

### 1.4 Proctoring Data (For Secure Exams)

When you take a proctored exam, we collect:

- **Video Recording:** Live video feed from your device camera for identity verification and behavior monitoring
- **Audio Recording:** Microphone input to detect unauthorized assistance
- **Screen Activity:** Screen captures and browser activity during the exam session
- **Behavioral Data:** Eye movement patterns, head position, environmental changes detected by AI
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates (only if required by your institution and with your consent)

### 1.5 Camera and Photo Library

- **Camera Access:** Used for identity verification, profile photos, and uploading visual content to exams
- **Photo Library:** Accessed to upload images for exam questions or save certificates

### 1.6 Biometric Data

- **Face ID/Touch ID:** Used for secure authentication and identity verification (stored locally on your device, not on our servers)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We only share your information in the following circumstances:

### 3.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or if necessary to:
- Comply with legal processes
- Protect our rights, property, or safety
- Prevent fraud or security threats
- Protect the safety of users or the public

### 3.4 Business Transfers
If ProficiencyAI is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

### 3.5 With Your Consent
We may share your information with other parties when you explicitly consent or direct us to do so.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active and for 2 years after account deletion (unless legally required to retain longer)
- **Exam Content:** Retained for the duration of the course/program plus 3 years for accreditation purposes
- **Proctoring Recordings:** Retained for 90 days after exam completion (or longer if flagged for review or involved in academic integrity investigation)
- **Anonymized Analytics:** Retained indefinitely for research and improvement purposes

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting support@proficiencyai.com. Note that some data may need to be retained for legal or institutional requirements.

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

### 5.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS 1.3 encryption
- **Encryption at Rest:** All stored data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- **Secure Authentication:** Passwords are hashed using bcrypt; support for biometric authentication
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access controls and multi-factor authentication for administrators

### 5.2 Organizational Safeguards
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Employee training on data privacy and security
- Data breach response plan and incident management procedures
- Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

### 5.3 Compliance
- FERPA compliant (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act)
- GDPR compliant (General Data Protection Regulation)
- COPPA compliant (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- SOC 2 Type II certified

**Note:** No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
